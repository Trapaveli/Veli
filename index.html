    Bio

    For inspiring artists, attempting to appeal within multiple markets there always seems to be the goal for these kids. After identifying certain era’s and taking in numerous genres all at once, the idea of “maturing your ear” seems to be the main narrative forgotten, while the act of imitating is prized. Baltimore’s CamQuarter is a breath of fresh air as someone so young and new in his career can maintain such maturity in his skills. Cam is sure of his sound, and his approach is strong. He possesses the ability to get on wide ranges of beats that span from LoFi R&B productions to general radio-friendly mashes and knows exactly how he wants it to come off. That admirable trait has allowed CQ to stir quite a buzz in a city that is progressively moving sonically. One of my personal favorite CamQuarter releases “Rembrandt” exhibits that raw talent with eleven tracks that allows him to sound as confident as possible, while also being merely introspective. With that method being the admirable possession today, you can look to see Cam continuing that technique being a sign of progression for artists following him the more he goes. Take a look at the talent above and get familiar with CamQuarter’s previous work available on all platforms.
